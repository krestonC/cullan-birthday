✦ HOW TO PLAY ✦
▶ START THE GAME

🎯 THE GOAL

Chop trees → Make planks → Craft pickaxes → Dig deeper → Find diamonds → Craft a Diamond Pickaxe to win!

A gold banner at the top of the screen tells Cullan exactly what to do next, at every step.

📱 ADD TO HOME SCREEN

Installs like a real app — icon on the home screen, runs full-screen, works without internet.

Android / Chrome

  1. Open the game link in Chrome
  2. Wait a few seconds — a glowing yellow 📱 INSTALL APP button appears top-right
  3. Tap it → "Add to home screen" → Install

iPhone / Safari

  1. Open the game link in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with up arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen → Add
After install, look for a green grass-block icon labelled "Block Quest" on the home screen. Tap once to launch full-screen.

🕹️ CONTROLS — ON A PHONE

D-pad in the bottom-left. Action buttons in the bottom-right.

  • ◄ ► ⏶ ▼ — move, jump, crouch
  • ⛏️ Mine the block in front of you. Or tap any block on the screen to mine it.
  • 🟩 Tap ONCE to "arm" place mode (button glows yellow), then tap where you want the block. Auto-disarms after 4 seconds.
  • SWAP cycle through blocks in the hotbar
  • CRAFT open the crafting menu
  • ⚡SSJ become Super Saiyan (required to fly)
  • ✈ toggle flight (only while Super Saiyan)
  • 💣 throw TNT in the direction you're facing

⌨️ CONTROLS — ON A COMPUTER

  • A D or ← → — move left/right
  • Space or W — jump
  • Left-click any block within reach to mine it
  • Right-click to place the selected block
  • G — go Super Saiyan (required to fly!)
  • F — toggle flight (while Super Saiyan, then W/S fly up/down)
  • Q — throw TNT
  • C — open crafting
  • 1–7 — switch hotbar slot
  • R — respawn after dying

⚡ SUPER SAIYAN CULLY

Press G (or tap ⚡SSJ) to transform into Super Saiyan. A golden flash covers the screen and "CULLY SUPER SAIYAN!!!!" rolls across in big letters.

While Super Saiyan:

  • Hair turns bright yellow and spiky ⚡
  • Glowing yellow aura follows Cully around
  • Flight is unlocked — press F (desktop) or ✈ (phone) then W/S or ⏶/▼ to fly up/down
  • Mines blocks 2× faster
  • Immune to lava and freezing
  • Jumps 40% higher when not flying

Press G (or tap ⚡SSJ) again to turn it off.

💣 TNT — BLOW STUFF UP

Press Q (or tap 💣) to throw a stick of TNT in the direction you're facing. It arcs through the air, lands, fuses for 1–2 seconds, then BOOM!

  • Destroys any block in a 3-block radius (except bedrock)
  • Kills any zombie or creeper in the blast
  • Does NOT hurt Cully — you can stand right on top of it!
  • Knocks Cully back for a fun ragdoll launch
Cully has unlimited TNT. Spam it freely to clear tunnels, destroy zombies, or just for fireworks.

🏗️ BUILDING — UNLIMITED BLOCKS

The first time Cully mines any block type (wood, stone, dirt, glass, leaves, planks, TNT) the inventory jumps to 999 (∞) of that block forever.

Placing blocks never uses them up. Build castles, towers, tunnels, floors — whatever, as much as he wants. The only way to place is:

  • Desktop: right-click anywhere within reach
  • Phone: tap 🟩 (it glows yellow) then tap the spot on the screen

⚠️ WARNINGS — WHAT CAN KILL YOU

🔥 LAVA zone (very deep) — past row 54 you start to burn. Touching an actual lava block does huge damage. Being Super Saiyan makes Cully immune to both.
❄️ FREEZING (very high) — near the top of the world it's too cold and you lose hearts slowly. Super Saiyan is immune.
🧟 ZOMBIES and CREEPERS at night. They chase you and deal 2–3 damage per hit. Best responses: fly away (Super Saiyan + F) or blow them up with TNT.

If Cully dies, press R on desktop or tap RESPAWN on the death screen on phone — he comes back at spawn with full hearts.

👨‍👩‍👦 FOR PARENTS

  • No downloads, no accounts, no ads. Opens in any web browser, installs as an app in one tap.
  • Offline-capable. Once opened once, the game works without wi-fi or data.
  • No chat, no multiplayer, no external links. It's a single-player sandbox — totally self-contained.
  • Save-safe. Progress is saved automatically on the device every few seconds. CONTINUE picks up where he left off; START QUEST begins a fresh world.
  • Kid-safe difficulty. TNT never hurts the player; Super Saiyan mode makes hazards non-lethal; unlimited blocks remove the grind.
  • No installation is needed — it's just a bookmark. If you prefer no home-screen icon, just open the link in a browser.
